About the UW FHIR® Interest Group

Who We Are

We are a group of UW students and faculty interested in the HL7 FHIR® standard as well as the surrounding technologies of SMART on FHIR, CDS Hooks, and Bulk FHIR Query. We organize tutorials and workshops and are currently the organizers for the Seattle on FHIR Meetup group that brings together students, researchers, industry professionals, and clinicians to discuss FHIR, showcase new applications, and collaborate on new projects.



Get Involved

Want to get involved? Join us once a month for the Seattle on FHIR Meetup. If you're new to FHIR, we have a collection of FHIR and SMART on FHIR tutorials as well as conference talks from our 2018 UW FHIR Workshop.
